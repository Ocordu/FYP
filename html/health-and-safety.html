Health and Safety
Desktop and Laptop Use
  • Food and drink should be consumed and stored well away from computers.
  • Any loose wires should be concealed to avoid damage or possible removal from sockets.
  • Regular 5-10 minute breaks should be taken when using a computer for an extended period of time.
  • Any vents should be left uncovered to avoid overheating.
  • Good posture, ideally with a straight back, should be maintained at all times.
  • Adjust the position of the keyboard and mouse to suit comfort and avoid wrist or arm discomfort.
  • Adjust the position of the monitor so the top of the screen is at approximately eye level, to avoid neck or shoulder discomfort.
  • Ensure that the working environment is comfortable eg: lighting and temperature.
  • Ensure glasses or equivalent are being worn during computer use.
  • Ensure that the screen is not too bright or too dark and is free from any flickering.
